Rubber Mass Ring Worker Tool

Worker-facing calculator for routed discharge line geometry, safe ring placement, resonance screening, and static screening.

This tool calculates recommended rubber mass ring layouts from full developed discharge line geometry. It uses measured natural frequency for design when entered, identifies safe straight sections automatically, and reports engineering screening results for placement, resonance risk, and static loading.
Fill the inputs, then press Calculate Recommendation.
