# Validation Plan

Qualify each model family/circuit/geometry revision on multiple physical units. Test startup, steady running and shutdown across the complete approved RPM, pressure and relevant temperature envelope. At every brazed joint, collect vertical and horizontal readings (and axial where required) with traceable instrument and calibration metadata. Record old-support baseline, trial configuration and final configuration as distinct conditions.

Evaluate repeatability and measurement uncertainty; inspect clamp preload, retention, contact condition, rubber durability and nearby-component interaction. Compare only compatible measurements under documented RPM, pressure, temperature, point, axis and instrument tolerances. Final acceptance requires each braze-axis pair to meet both 50% reduction against the old-support baseline and the approved absolute velocity criterion. A failed or missing axis invalidates the configuration.
